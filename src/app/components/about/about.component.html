Our Philosophy

Our mission is to promote sustainable travel by providing insights into the environmental impact of flights. We believe that by being aware of our carbon footprint, we can make more responsible choices and reduce our impact on the planet.

Through this app, we aim to empower travelers with data, encouraging eco-conscious decisions without compromising on the joy of discovery. Join us in making the world a cleaner, greener place, one journey at a time.

- The Travel Impact Team
